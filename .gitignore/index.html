Welcome to MY VIRTUAL RUNNING COMPANION

We want to present you a simple and and easy to use weight tracker tool that keeps track of your weight daily like a journal. You can set a goal and check your current BMI. MY VIRTUAL RUNNING COMPANION also show you the history data that you can visually see your progress and help you manage your weight more effectively.
